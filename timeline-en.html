Data Requirements for Calculation
System Component Required Data Probable Source Status
Pricing Logic
Inspection Services Catalog of all inspections, categories, typical effort LPV, inspectors Pending
Price Items Base flat rates, unit prices, reference features (€/device, €/detector, ...) LPV, offers Pending
Volume Tiers Volume discounts (e.g. from 500 devices → €3.80 instead of €4.50) LPV, offers Pending
Surcharges Express (+25%), night (+30%), legacy system (+20%), ATEX (+35%), medical (+25%) Offers, calculation spreadsheets Pending
Regions & Travel Costs Regional price factors, distance flat rates Internal rules, offers Pending
Buildings & Installations
Building Types Office, industrial, hospital, high-rise, etc. — with mapping to mandatory inspections Standards (LBO), inspectors Pending
Building & Installation Characteristics Gross floor area, floors, number of devices, detectors, down-conductors, protection class, ... Inspection reports, RFQs Partially available
Hazard Zones ATEX, medical, high voltage, wet area — triggers & surcharges Standards (ATEX, LBO), inspectors Pending
Usage Types Office, industrial, public, residential — determines inspection intervals Standards, inspectors Pending
Rule Set & Logic
Mandatory Inspections Which inspections are legally required per building type Standards (LBO, DGUV, DIN), inspectors Pending
Standards & Regulations DGUV V3, BetrSichV, DIN EN 62305, LBO, ATEX, VdS CEA, ... Inspection reports, publicly available Partially available
Inspection Intervals Semi-annually to 4 years — depending on usage type & service Inspection reports, standards Partially available
Qualification Requirements EFK, PSV, ZÜS, qualified person — per inspection & hazard zone Standards, inspectors Pending
Estimation Formulas Gross floor area → number of down-conductors, detectors, devices (real proportions) Inspection reports, inspectors Calibration possible
Cost Drivers & Effects Why does X cost more? (special building, legacy system, time pressure, ...) Inspectors, offers Pending
Cross-Selling & Bundling
Bundle Discounts Which inspections share site visits → discount (e.g. fire alarm + sprinkler = 10%) Offers, inspectors Pending
Recommendations Upsell logic: which inspections are additionally recommended Inspectors, offers Pending

Inspection reports (sample reports) are already available. What's primarily missing for calculation is pricing data (LPV, offers, calculation spreadsheets) and rule set validation by inspectors.
